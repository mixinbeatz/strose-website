WELCOME

A Home for
Every Soul

Saint Rose Philippine Duchesne Parish is a vibrant Catholic community in Anthem, Arizona. Named after the first American to be canonized west of the Mississippi, our parish carries forward her legacy of faith, perseverance, and devotion to the Sacred Heart.

Whether you are a lifelong Catholic, exploring the faith, or returning home — you are welcome here.
